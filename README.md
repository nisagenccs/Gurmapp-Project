# Gurmapp ☕📍

**Gurmapp** is a mobile application concept developed by students from TOBB University of Economics and Technology (TOBB ETÜ).

The goal of the application is to help users easily discover the best cafés, restaurants, and local food spots around them.

## 🚀 Project Vision

Finding a good place to eat or drink coffee can be difficult, especially in large cities or unfamiliar neighborhoods.

Gurmapp aims to simplify this process by creating a community-based platform where users can discover, explore, and share their favorite cafés and restaurants.

The application focuses on helping users:

- discover nearby cafés and restaurants  
- view menus and price ranges  
- save favorite places  
- share photos and experiences  
- explore hidden local gems  

## ✨ Key Features

- 📍 Location-based café and restaurant discovery  
- ⭐ Favorite places system  
- 📸 User-generated photos and reviews  
- 📖 Menu and price visibility  
- 🔎 Personalized recommendations
